Water pumping solutions

DOMESTIC BOOSTER PUMPS

These are pumps suitable for pumping water in domestic application with options for surface centrifugal pumps used for inline pressure boosting.

VERTICAL MULTISTAGE PUMPS

These are high performance pumps with multiple impellers suitable in areas where continuous duty is required such as domestic pumping, irrigation, industrial and civil application.

BOOSTER SETS

Suitable for domestic and commercial application where more than one pump system is required. We offer customized booster sets with automatic controls which ensure continuous water supply and improved pump performance.

SUBMERSIBLE PUMPS

We offer a wide range of submersible well pumps suitable for wells with depths of up to 100 meters with AC and DC options. The pumps offer high performance efficiency with impellers designed to pump water with high sand content.

SOLAR WATER PUMPS

Solar pumping is now firmly established as a technology for water supply in remote off-grid applications and also as the cost reduce increasingly for general pumping duties.

SEWAGE/EFFLUENT PUMPS

Our submersible drainage pumps are designed to drain waste water.Their impellers can withstand water with high solid and fiber content ensuring efficient performance.

DRAINAGE PUMPS

Our submersible drainage pumps are designed to drain waste water from basement and other areas that are prone to flooding .Their impellers can withstand water with high impurities ensuring efficient performance.

CIRCULATOR PUMPS

For closed circuit systems solar water heaters and machines for domestic and industrial application, the pumps can handle cold and hot water.

CHEMICAL DOSING PUMPS

Dosing pumps are high specification products for the accurate dosage of a wide range of chemicals in general water treatment and industrial process applications.

About Us

Our Brand Story

Norwa Africa was established with the purpose to provide reliable water treatment and water pumping solutions in Africa. We endeavour to address the water scarcity, the challenging water quality as well as the growing demand for green energy through solar solutions and water treatment machines.
Since its establishment, Norwa Africa has been on a journey, seeking solutions in water pumping, solar power, water treatment for African communities and governments.
With the growing water scarcity, need for renewable energy, and the numerous water quality concerns in Africa, Norwa Africa dedicates itself to provide reliable water treatment solutions in the most professional approach, to uplift our communities.
We are proud of its success and look to the future with enthusiasm, to grow with the needs of our customers, to transform communities and change lives.
