Double filter system

Get access to clean water at home with double the filtration

The double filter system is an energy saving, environmentally friendly, small size and easy operation water treatment machine.Their are two types undersink and table top

Contact more information us →

Basic Features

  • Water Flow : 1.5m/Min
  • Filter Type : Simple Filter with Sediment or Block Carbon
  • Dimensions : 590*310*350mm

Product Features

  • Small size and easy operation
  • Filters can either:
    • Sediment filter to remove dirt, rust other solid objects or,
    • Carbon filter that takes out 98% of the chlorine and organic chemicals, herbicides, pesticides or,
    • Block activated carbon a fine sediment reduction for removal of odor, taste, cyst sand turbidity.
  • Energy saving, environment protection
