DKm Horizontal Pumps

Electronic water pump, commercial electric water pump

DKm pumps are recommended for pumping water and chemically non-aggressive liquid. With an open impeller, DK pumps are designed pumping fairly large amounts of impurities without fault cogging. suitable for irrigation, industrial applications, etc. As with impeller cantilevered on the motor shaft directly, the DK pumps have a compact size, very reliable, simple to use and almost maintenance-free. It can be installed horizontally or vertically.

PKm Horizontal Pumps

Electronic water pump, commercial electric water pump

The Pkm range of peripheral impeller booster pumps are robust, reliable and economical and used principally for water boosting in domestic and small scale water transfer applications where there is insufficient mains pressure available. The pump body is made from cast iron with a brass insert set into the front housing for improved starting performance and protection from jamming.
